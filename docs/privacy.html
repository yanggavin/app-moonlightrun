Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

Data We Collect

MoonlightRun collects location data only during active run sessions to calculate route, distance, pace, and workout history.

How We Use Data

We use run data to provide core app functionality. We do not sell personal data and do not use your data for advertising.

Storage and Sync

Data is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud Sync, data is synced with your private iCloud account via CloudKit.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data by contacting support@moonlightrun-app.newtree.tech.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@moonlightrun-app.newtree.tech.
